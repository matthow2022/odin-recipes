Expensive fun night

Description

When I am under the influence and want something expensive, I have food from Carrabba's delivered to me.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Bryan
  • 2 sides of fettuccine
  • Bread and oil
  • Coke

Steps

  1. When I am under the influence, I pull out my phone to place an order for delivery from Carrabba's.
  2. I order Chicken Bryan, fettuccine, bread and oil, and a Coke
  3. I wait for the food to be dropped off and tip the driver.
  4. Once I get the food, I eat it and enjoy the rest of the night.
